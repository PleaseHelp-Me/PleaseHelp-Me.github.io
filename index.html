My life in my home country

I lost my family when I was a little kid. I was working at people's home just to let me stay at night under a roof and a little safe. Working and living homeless town to town until when I was 18 years old. Then I went to the army. I was 5 years in the army and it was the best moment of my life that I had a place to stay and food until I lost my arm in a explosion and I could not stay in the army anymore.

Homeless, town to town, country to country, what a sad and small world

People laugh on me why I have torn clothes, why I am so thin, why I have black hairs, why I always walk around street to street, town to town ... No problem, I with a sad heart love to make people laugh on me. I love to see laugh and smile on each face in every person in the entire world. Let them laugh, if they have experienced a tiny piece of my problems then they had never laughed on me. I come to you with a big hope because I know that you are kindhearted enough to feel me and understand me.
